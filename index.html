Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about the application portal, timelines, or qualifications? We have answers.

Aether is open to college students, developers, UI/UX designers, and industry professionals from all over the world. Whether you're an absolute beginner or an expert, we invite you to build with us.

Teams can range from 1 to 4 individuals. If you do not have a team prior to registering, don't worry! You can use our interactive Team Matcher on the portal or attend our virtual team-forming mixer on Day 1.

Registration and participation are free for most cohorts. However, some specialized hackathons may require a registration fee. We supply API credits, developer tooling keys, digital workspace tools, and standard swag items to all approved hackers.

No. All projects must be started and built during the designated 36-hour hacking window. Pre-existing templates and public boilerplate structures may be used, but all core implementation logic must be coded on-site. Commit hashes are tracked.

Projects are evaluated on three major metrics: Engineering Complexity, Usability & Product Design, and Originality. The final score is an average from an expert panel of startup founders, tech leads, and product designers.
